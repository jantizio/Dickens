Re-enactment of Charles Dickens' visit to Certosa in collaboration with the Association 8cento carried out by students of classes 5Bi of the Belluzzi-Fioravanti High School and 5U of the Righi High School in the 2020-21 school year. Dickens (Gianmarco Mazzoni, 5Bi) and the caretaker-guide Marcellino (Marco Maglione, 5U) in the Chapel of Suffrages.
