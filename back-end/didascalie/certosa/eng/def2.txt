View of the Sala delle Tombe, designed by Angelo Venturoli (1749-1821) in 1816, re-using structures from the Sala di Ricreazione and other rooms of the Carthusian monastery. the setting is scenographically closed by the monumento mariri for independence by Carlo Monari (1867 ca.)
