A view of the Chiostro Terzo, to the north, from the portico of the Cappella de Suffragi. The engraving was part of an ambitious editorial project by Marcellino Sibaud that was never completed; "The municipal cemetery of Bologna: an archaeological-historical-descriptive work" (1837). The intention was to create a real history of funerary traditions in seven volumes, to reach the Bolognese cemetery
