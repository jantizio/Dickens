Re-enactment of Dickens' visit to the Hotel del Pellegrino in collaboration with the 8th century APS Association and the Museum of the Risorgimento in Bologna. Dickens in the centre (Gianmarco Mazzoni, 5Bi), the Waiter on the right (Lorenzo Fabbri, 5U), another guest of the Hotel on the left (Felipe Lamberti, 5Bi).
